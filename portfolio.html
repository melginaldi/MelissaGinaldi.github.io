Leadership Experience

Teaching Assistant

I have been a teaching assistant for Cornell's intro to data structures class for 3 semesters. During my time as a TA I co-led 12 recitation sections per semester teaching new data structures and algorithms in Java to 30+ students. I also guided them through problem sets and created weekly study guides and presentations to aid their learning. Other respobsilibities I had included holding weekly office hours for 2+ hours a week, grading 3 different exams for the 600+ student class, and grading coding projects for the students in my discussion section.

This experience taught me how to communicate effectively and how to work well with others.

Peer Advisor:

I volunteered to be a peer advisor for the college of engineering in order to help new freshman adjust to Cornell. I co-facilitated a freshman 10-week seminar to help students with their transition into college by presenting and explaining important topics such as grading and course enrollment. As a peer advisor I also collaborated with faculty and other Cornell staff to coordinate 3 guest lectures.
