Lessons & Growth

"Every success story is a tale of constant adaptation, revision, and change"

- Richard Branson

It's okay to make mistakes!

Every mistake is really just a learning opportunity. I shouldn't shy away from failure, I should chase it beacuse that is when I will be pushing myself the most.

Teamwork is everything

Software engineering is designed to be done with teams not indiviuals. There is so much I don't know, and what I have never been exposed to my teammates might have been. In order to build the best product I need all of the collaboration I can get

Company Culture

Although my professional expereince has been short in years, I have learned how important company culture is to me. A good work environment with a great boss and team makes me twice as productive and passionate about my work. Even if the work may not be the most interesting on its own, being on a team thats excited all togeteher will make the work interesting.

Adaptaion and Change

Between being a college student and COVID-19 I have learned more than ever how to adapt to new situations and work environments. Before coming to college, one of my greatest weaknesses was my stubborness to change. I like routines but I have now found a way to enjoy my routines while also embracing any revisions they face.

Effective Leading

Being a leader is about being able to listen. I have a lot more room to grow but throughout my time at Cornell this lesson has constantly stuck with me. An effective leader listens first and goes above and beyond for their team.

New Technologies I've learned in 2021

Over the course of this past year I got to learn and work with Kubernetes, Docker and Helm Chart for the first time. I also created my own Makefile and website using html and css which I had never done before as well.
